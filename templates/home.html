OUR APPROACH


Millions of girls and women are currently ‘invisible,’ preventing them from fully participating in their communities. Equal Measures 2030 envisions a world where no girl or woman is invisible. We connect data and evidence with advocacy and action, helping to fuel progress towards gender equality. That means making sure girls’ and women’s movements, rights advocates and decision makers have easy-to-use data and evidence to guide efforts to reach the Global Goals by 2030.
